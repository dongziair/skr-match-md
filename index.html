SKR Match

SKR Match is a Solana-based match-3 puzzle game. Players connect a Solana wallet to save progress, appear on the leaderboard, claim free daily in-game bonuses, and optionally purchase in-game power-ups with SKR tokens.

SKR Match does not provide investment services, yield, gambling, cash prizes, or financial advice. In-game purchases are non-refundable on-chain token transfers for game power-ups only.
