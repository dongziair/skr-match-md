Privacy Policy for SKR Match

Last Updated: April 29, 2026

SKR Match ("App", "we", "us", "our") is a Solana-based match-3 puzzle game. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and how you can manage or delete your data. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data required for the App to function:

  • Public Solana wallet address.
  • Game progress, current level, inventory, and daily bonus timestamp.
  • Leaderboard display name (resolved .skr domain or shortened wallet address).
  • Public on-chain transaction data for wallet-confirmed purchases.
  • Leaderboard reports submitted by users.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, access, store, or request:

  • Private keys, seed phrases, or wallet recovery phrases.
  • Real names, email addresses, phone numbers, or mailing addresses.
  • Government-issued identification documents.
  • Payment card or bank account data.
  • Health data, biometric data, or precise geolocation.
  • Device contacts, photos, or files.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Saving and restoring game progress across sessions.
  • Managing in-game inventory and daily bonuses.
  • Displaying and maintaining the leaderboard.
  • Processing user-initiated on-chain SKR token transfers for in-game power-ups.
  • Reviewing user reports and preventing abuse.
  • Improving App stability and performance.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required by applicable law in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under the applicable age, we will take steps to delete such data promptly. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided data to us, please contact us at limindong01@gmail.com.

5. Wallet Transactions

Optional in-game power-up purchases require a Solana Mainnet transaction approved by the user's wallet. Before requesting wallet approval, the App clearly displays the item, token amount, recipient address, network, and the non-refundable nature of the purchase.

Solana blockchain transactions are public and permanent. SKR Match cannot delete, modify, or reverse public blockchain transaction history.

6. User-Generated Content

Leaderboard display names may be derived from user-controlled .skr domains. Users can report inappropriate leaderboard names directly within the App. Users can also hide individual leaderboard entries. We moderate reported content to ensure compliance with our Terms of Use and applicable law.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices, including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS), to protect data transmitted between the App and our servers. While we take reasonable measures to safeguard your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Deletion

You can delete your SKR Match server profile and local device data at any time by opening Privacy & Safety within the App and choosing Delete Wallet Data. The deletion request requires a wallet-signed message to verify ownership.

Deletion removes all SKR Match profile data associated with your wallet from our servers. It does not remove public Solana blockchain transaction history, which is immutable by design.

9. Third-Party Services

The App may integrate with or rely on the following third-party services and infrastructure:

  • Solana-compatible wallets and Solana Mobile Wallet Adapter.
  • Solana RPC providers for blockchain communication.
  • Jupiter price APIs for token price display.
  • Related Web3 infrastructure services.

These third-party services may process public blockchain data or technical request metadata under their own privacy policies. We require that any third-party service that may access user data through the App complies with applicable data protection laws and standards consistent with this Privacy Policy.

10. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide the App's features or as required by applicable law. When you request data deletion, we will delete your data promptly, except where retention is required for legitimate legal or regulatory purposes (such as fraud prevention).

11. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at limindong01@gmail.com or use the in-app data deletion feature described in Section 8.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates the most recent revision. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact

For privacy, safety, leaderboard, data deletion, or transaction concerns, contact us at: limindong01@gmail.com
